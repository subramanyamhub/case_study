What We Do

360 Degree Car Wash

We wash cars with pressure washer from every corner leaving no dirt or mud. Even the underbody is also cleaned with the long underbody extension.

Foam Wash

We perform foam wash on cars. Dense foam is good for car paints and protect the paint from scratches and UV rays. Foam wash gives a shiny appearance to car exterior.

Interior Cleaning

We perform interior cleaning with industry graded Vacuum Cleaner and use protectant and heavy duty cleaning liquid to remove stains from car interior sections like roof, side doors and glasses.


Paint Protection

Paint protection service is something which can protect the exterior paint from UV rays, dust particles and hard water stains.

Exterior Polish

Polish is used to help the car exterior shine and give a showroom finish look. Polish also provides the exterior paint a hydrophobic nature which does not allow water to stay on the car exterior.

Scratch Removal

We can remove scratches and alien paints from the car exterior with heavy duty rubbing compound.
